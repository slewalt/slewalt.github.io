About Me

I am currently a studio & store manager for a jewelry designer with a 3D installation artist back ground, living in Seattle, WA. Oh, and I make & design custom pinatas. I became interested in Front-End Web Design and Development because I want to understand how to format & design websites. I’ve taken a stab at customizing websites built on an already existing template, but mainly through googling a series of questions until something reacts the way I want it to. I want to understand the structure & function of websites & how I can combine that with design.

My Mission

As I learn more about front-end, I hope to one day work as a self supported artist and free-lance web designer working with other artists or entrepreneurs that need help with branding & a place to showcase their work. I like to work in an environment that is just beginning & communicating the vision to the world through design.
